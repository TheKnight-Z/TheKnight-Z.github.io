Research interest

My research interests mainly lie at the intersection of Computer Vision, Robotics and Machine Learning.

I am especially interested in how we humans perceive various information from physical world, and then subsequently plan and move accordingly. My goal is to transfer this knowledge to machines, aiming to build interactive machines that can help us explore physical world and expand our boundaries of perception, decision and action.
